Get involved with Project Lacewing

Project Lacewing is looking for people who want to contribute to a European, independent answer to AI-driven vulnerability research. There are three ways to get involved:

Technical

Help think through the technical setup: which AI models to use, what hardware is needed, which software targets to tackle first. We're looking for people with experience in security research, vulnerability disclosure, AI/ML, or systems administration.

Email us

Fundraising

Help raise funds. We're looking for people who can write grant applications, have connections with potential partners, or have experience attracting donors for non-profit initiatives.

Email us

Champion

Help champion Project Lacewing. We're looking for people who want to co-lead this initiative, represent it externally, and help embed it within DIVD and the broader European cybersecurity community.

Email us

Financial support

Project Lacewing is a non-profit initiative. Every contribution goes directly toward AI capacity and research.
